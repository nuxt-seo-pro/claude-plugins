# Output Formats

Artifact templates for research outputs.

## target-keywords.md

```markdown
---
generated: [date]
topic: [seed topic]
---

# Target Keywords

## Primary Keywords

| Keyword | Volume | Difficulty | CPC | Priority |
|---------|--------|------------|-----|----------|
| [keyword] | [monthly] | [0-100] | [$X.XX] | high/medium/low |

## Long-tail Opportunities

| Keyword | Volume | Difficulty | Content Gap |
|---------|--------|------------|-------------|
| [keyword] | [monthly] | [0-100] | [why worth targeting] |

## Question Keywords

| Question | Volume | Answer Status |
|----------|--------|---------------|
| [how/what/why...] | [monthly] | missing/partial/exists |
```
